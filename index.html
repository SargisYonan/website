ABOUT

Computer, Software and Robotics Engineer

I’m an engineer who works on a wide breadth of projects. I enjoy building hardware, writing firmware, kernel drivers, system software—and even back-end and front-end web development.

I also work in the Assyrian language space where I contribute to Unicode CLDR, design OpenType fonts, create Assyrian word games, and various learning tools. More recently, I've been interested in computational linguistics in the field of the Assyrian language.

I’m a Camera Systems Engineer at Apple Inc.
