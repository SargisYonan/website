Assyrian Language Projects

Mamlal
A react-app based Assyrian language Wordle clone. The word list was scraped from the Kaikki Assyrian Neo-Aramaic dictionary (previously assyrianlanguages.org).
mamlal.com

AssyrianPhonetics
A simple computer speach to text tool for Assyrian (NENA dialect).
assyrianphonetics.github.io

Iqranita
A root-word flash card game to test knowledge of Assyrian/Semitic root words. Data scraped from assyrianlanguages.org.
assyrianphonetics.github.io/iqranita

The Nohadra Syriac Fonts Collection
Nohadra fonts are a bold, geometric, and monospaced Syriac typeface. The Nohadra Syriac Font family includes Syriac typefaces with square, block-like characters and uniform line thickness. This font offers a modern and minimalistic design and feel.
